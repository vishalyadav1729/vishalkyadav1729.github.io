Projects

Applied and Educational Projects

Predicting 30-Day Hospital Readmissions

A data science project in which I developed a machine-learning framework to predict 30-day hospital readmissions using a comprehensive synthetic electronic health record dataset. The project involved comparing interpretable and ensemble models to identify patients at highest risk and support more targeted post-discharge care.

Accessive Learning for K–2 Students

A fully funded educational initiative by the Learning Disabilities Association of Newfoundland and Labrador aimed at improving learning outcomes for children with disabilities through technology integration. As a Curriculum Developer, I conducted research on evidence-based instructional methods and helped design a research-informed curriculum for early learners.
