I’m Vishal Yadav, a PhD candidate in Mathematics at Memorial University (GPA 4.0), focusing on Lie and Hopf algebras. I completed an Integrated BS–MS at IISER TVM, and my research experience spans analytic number theory, sieve methods, and operator theory (von Neumann extensions). I value research and teaching equally. I serve as a Teaching Assistant at MUN and have been a Guest Faculty at the University of Delhi and Deen Dayal Upadhyaya College, teaching Advanced Group Theory, Real Analysis, and Mathematical Finance. I’ve presented talks on the Cyclic group gradings in prime characteristic, Prime Number Theorem and Sieve theory, and I actively participate in algebra and CGT workshops and conferences Outside of math, I’m a drummer, trekker, and poetry enthusiast.

I’m open to Data analyst positions,research collaborations, teaching opportunities, and mentorship. Feel free to reach out.
